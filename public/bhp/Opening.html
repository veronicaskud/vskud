Opening: Life of a Star

Opening: Life of a star

Download the Life of Star worksheet.

Print out the Life of a Star worksheet or project the set of images detailing the life of a star (we’re assuming that you’re using printouts, but you can easily alter the instructions for the exercise if you’re projecting the images). Have students use the worksheet to arrange the images from the beginning to the end of the life of a star. At this point, the students are not presumed to have any formal understanding of how stars form. This activity provides an opportunity to start thinking about this process and to put their limited understanding to use. This activity should be hands-on and very quick. Later in the lesson, they will have a chance to form a deeper understanding of how stars form.
