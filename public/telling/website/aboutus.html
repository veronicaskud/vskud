We are educational technologists, film-makers, and storytellers

Our Story

We are a Boulder-based hub for mobile media creativity, guided by a mission to revive the craft of storytelling as instruction. IN THE TELLING is a software company and boutique studio, transforming lectures into documentaries, educators into producers, and professors into stars of their own movies.

Our Team

IN THE TELLING has a cast of creative talent from award winning film-makers, animators, graphic artists, usability experience designers, programmers, mobile App developers and eLearning pioneers that bring together decades of experience creating films, educational media and learning management systems. Together we are engaged in the producing the Art of Documentary Instruction through the TELLING Technologies and Services.
