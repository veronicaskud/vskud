We are educational technologists, filmmakers, and storytellers.

Our Story

The first time we turned on an iPad we realized the prevailing narrative had changed from text to video. We partnered with the Leeds School of Business, filmed the sustainable business case stories of Namaste Solar and programmed the first transmedia App for IOS and Android tablets. We've since evolved those Apps into a cloud streaming platform, and expanded our educator partnerships to innovate documentary instruction on the mobile media devices students use.

Our Leadership

Jeff Larsen

CEO

Jeff has been building core eLearning technologies and media companies since 1988. He has a film degree from NYU, a Ph.D. ABD at CU Boulder, and is an award winning filmmaker and media developer. He built one of the first object oriented database and media driven learning systems in the early 90’s, Coursenet Systems. He founded IN THE TELLING in 2009. Two patents are pending for the TELLING Player and Platform.

Kevin Johnson

Client-Partner Relationships & Communications

Kevin is an entrepreneur and imagineer who has been starting new eLearning ventures since the early 90’s. He helped build the Prentice Hall new media group and was the first VP of Business Development at Real Education (eCollege) and contributed to its 1999 IPO. He joined Jeff Larsen in 2010 to help build IN THE TELLING’s partner and client base. Kevin manages the strategic partnership, business development, sales and marketing efforts at IN THE TELLING.

Ray Henderson

Outside Director / Investor

Ray Henderson is an investor and a member of our Board of Directors. He is currently a senior executive with Blackboard, a leading education platform company. Ray’s an active open standards advocate and is a board advisor to the IMS global standards organization. Prior to that he held executive positions at Pearson Education, eCollege, and ANGEL Learning.

Our Team

IN THE TELLING has a cast of filmmakers, animators, graphic artists, user experience designers, programmers, mobile App developers and eLearning pioneers. Together we are engaged in defining the Art of Documentary Instruction.
