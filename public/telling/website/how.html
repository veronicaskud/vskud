The things we care about

  • Instruction Produced

    Cinematic instruction responsively delivered through mobile media devices from phones, tablets, to laptops & desktops creates freedom to engage instruction wherever, whenever.

  • The Art of Engagement

    What if instruction was produced to be seen, heard, touched, experienced? What if lecture had choice where a student could read, listen, watch, unfold visualizations, share and exchange?

  • Educational Standards

    LMS’s have proliferated globally to manage blended and distance education. The TELLING Player seamlessly integrates with LTI compliant LMS’s interoperating and exchanging transactional data with their course admin functionality bringing documentary instruction to online learning.

  • Easy Authoring Tools

    TELLING Producer helps educators develop, assemble and publish mobile-device ready video narratives that raise the bar on engaging students in blended learning, flipped classroom, and distance education. Educators can build and evolve their documentary instruction productions.

  • Cloud Streaming & Scale

    The player and content management system stream from the world’s most scalable, cloud network managed by thePlatform, Akamai and Amazon’s EC2 streaming service. We auto-detect mobile-media devices, bandwidth and browsers delivering consistent video, courseware experiences to leading browsers.

  • Creative Services

    IN THE TELLING producers and course designers work with instructors to plan and develop their documentary instruction productions, courseware and instructor offline bio shorts. We become partners in producing your instruction for mobile-media learners with as little burden or disruption to faculty as possible.
