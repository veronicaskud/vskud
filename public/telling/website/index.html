We bring the art of storytelling to online courses. Great stories engage the mind and heart, sparking curiosity and the desire to learn.

Our innovation is Documentary Instruction, an alchemy of documentary film, instructional design, transmedia curation and mobile-first technologies.

In The Telling

We are a Boulder-based hub for mobile media creativity, guided by a mission to revive the craft of storytelling as instruction. IN THE TELLING is a software company and boutique studio, transforming lectures into documentaries, educators into producers, and professors into stars in cinematic lectures.
