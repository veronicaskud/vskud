We help educators create more cinematic lectures, using mobile-first media.

Instruction Produced

We’ve all had priceless moments of insight provoked by a masterful storyteller. We’ve had these experiences live, but also when watching documentary films—a medium often under-appreciated for its instructional power. We bring the talent and technology to help educators harness this power, and distribute new digital narratives in a cinematic format optimized for playback on mobile devices.

The Art of Engagement

Too much of the instructional content provided to learners today fails to inspire. By remixing instructional delivery with popular consumer technology formats, we tilt the odds in favor of educators vying for attention. The result is highly produced, more convenient for concept review, and more captivating as a performance. We believe these are meaningful contributions to improve student engagement.

Technology

We assume the presence of an education platform, likely a Learning Management System. Our finished works integrate seamlessly with any modern LMS compliant with open standards for learning tool interoperability. We supplement LMS platforms with film-industry grade cloud video distribution to ensure scaled playback by learners.

Course Format

Our experience suggests a convergence of requirements across what were once darker lines separating “traditional” and “distance” courses. Some are delivered purely online. Others may flip a classroom using instructional video as part of that strategy. Our approach is agnostic about format.

Creative Services

IN THE TELLING producers and course designers consult with instructors to plan and develop their course productions. We film, edit, animate and integrate instruction for mobile-media learners with minimal disruption to faculty workload.
