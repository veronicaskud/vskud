The last mile in online education is the lecture

Education delivery is undergoing a great deal of change. Learners are more discerning of quality and more demanding of convenience than ever. And while nearly every learner is now reached by some form of learning platform, this brings administrative convenience, rather than material benefit to the instructional content available. Far too often the instructional content— the lecture material—delivered via the LMS is a poor shadow of the potential of the instructor.

“We are, as a species, addicted to story. Even when the body goes to sleep, the mind stays up all night, telling itself stories.”

Johnathan Gottschall
The Story Telling Animal: How Stories Make Us Human

In The Telling is a company founded to develop instructional delivery to the fullest potential. While “lecture capture” technology may bring a facsimile of the lecture to students, it can’t help but bring along the limitations of the live medium. Our objectives are to bring the powerful instructional benefits of both the mediums of film and mobile media, but also the art form of documentary storytelling , to the artisans telling the stories.
